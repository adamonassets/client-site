Ambassador Agreement

1. Program Overview

United All Around ("UAA") invites selected individuals ("Ambassadors") to participate in our Ambassador Program. By submitting this application and accepting these terms, you agree to the following conditions.

2. Eligibility

  • You must have a public Instagram account.
  • Applicants who are minors must have consent from a parent or legal guardian.
  • You must be located in the United States.

3. What You Receive

  • Up to 2 complimentary UAA gymnastics leotards.
  • A unique discount code (15% off) to share with your followers.
  • Potential features on UAA's official Instagram and website.

4. Your Commitments

  • Post a minimum of 2 Instagram posts (feed posts or Reels) featuring UAA leotards within 30 days of receiving your product.
  • Tag @united_all_around in each post and caption.

This program provides product-based compensation only. No monetary payment is guaranteed or implied unless separately agreed upon in writing.

7. Product Terms

  • Leotards provided are complimentary and non-returnable.

  • Products are for personal use and may not be resold.

8. Termination

Either party may end the ambassador relationship at any time with no obligation. UAA reserves the right to remove ambassadors who do not meet posting requirements or whose content does not align with brand values.

9. Disclaimer
